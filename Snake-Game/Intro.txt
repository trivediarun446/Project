Snake Game 


Problem Statement :- 

    Construct a Snake game which take user input for moving or changing his directions  and also increase the size of snake 



Solution :- 


Step wise procedure :- 


1) Construct a method name frame . Which consist of frame of the game . frame means the area in which snake will move 

2) Construct a method which show the initial position of snake and food . 

3) Their is also the method name input . This function take user input for changing the directions of the snake . 

4) Another method name random which help food take diffrent diffrent position after eatting by the snake 

5) Their is a last function which help in for increase the lenght of the snake and snake body follow the head of the snake
